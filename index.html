Rocking Blues

Three guitars with a tight bass and backbeat says it all. Shakedown Collective mix their own songs with rock, rhythm and blues dance-floor fillers. Expect a rockin' show!

Performing the music of....

  • Lou Reed

  • Jimi Hendrix

  • Muddy Waters

  • Rolling Stones

  • John Lee Hooker

and their own original rocking compositions
