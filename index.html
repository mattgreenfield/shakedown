Rocking Blues

The Shakedown Collective are a unique, high-energy rocking blues band sporting their own brand of foot-tapping funky, melodic originals mixed in with some classic blues covers to get everybody involved.

With roots of Muddy Waters, licks akin to The Jimi Hendrix Experience, a modern spin of addictive catchy riffs and emotional harmonies The Shakedown Collective have an energy sure to get you out of your seat and take notice.
